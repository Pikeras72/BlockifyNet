Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

About the Model

Q: How does BlockifyNet work?
A: BlockifyNet uses a Multiclass PatchGAN model. It generates images that resemble Lego® Star Wars characters based on the input image you provide.
Q: What kind of images can I generate?
A: You can generate images in various Star Wars categories such as Wookies, Togrutas, or even famous characters of the saga. The generated images are inspired by the Lego® Star Wars saga.

Using the Website

Q: How do I generate an image?
A: Navigate to the homepage and select a category from the grid. Clicking on a category will take you to a page where you can upload your image and generate a new one.
Q: Can I download the generated images?
A: Yes, you can download the generated images. After the model has processed your image, a button will appear allowing you to download it directly to your device. This way, you can easily save and share your creations.

Technical Questions

Q: What happens if I encounter an error?
A: If you encounter an issue while using the website, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact us through the project's GitHub page (Pikeras72's Github).
Q: What technologies were used to build BlockifyNet?
A: BlockifyNet is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Pix2Pix AI model among others. The model is trained with a multiclass PatchGAN discriminator to generate detailed images.

About the Project

Q: Who is behind BlockifyNet?
A: BlockifyNet was developed by Diego Ruiz Piqueras, along with contributions from Emil Stelian Pintilie and Manuel Doblado Bueno, all students at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid.
Q: How did the project start?
A: The project began as a university collaboration and evolved into a personal project aimed at making AI accessible globally by providing tools for image generation.
Q: Can I contribute to the project?
A: Yes! You can contribute to the project by visiting the GitHub repository at Pikeras72's Github.
